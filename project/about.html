OUR

STORY

The Joyalukkas group has rapidly expanded its foothold all over the globe since its inception in 1987. Joyalukkas is today a trusted brand in India, UK, USA, Singapore, Malaysia, UAE, Qatar, Kuwait, KSA, Bahrain & Oman. The brand is continuing to rapidly expand its presence in more countries across the world to fulfill its vision of Ornamenting the World
